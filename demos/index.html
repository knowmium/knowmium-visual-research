The Core Interaction
In all three variations, clicking on a speaker turn reveals a layer underneath — the analysis slides open between the turns, as if the screen is parting to show what's beneath the surface of the conversation. Pivotal moments are marked with yellow indicators and expand to show richer context.
The Right Panel
The right panel is a grid of pivotal moment categories — Opening Moves, Posture Shifts, Breakthroughs, Friction Points, Commitments, Insights. Clicking any category illuminates it and expands a detail view showing the specific instances with timestamps and quotes.
What's Intentionally Unresolved
The specific category labels in the right panel grid are placeholders — the final set of pivotal moment types needs to be defined. The number of tiles, their icons, and their names are all open for iteration.
Next Steps
Pick a structural direction (or hybrid), define the final pivotal moment categories, and we scaffold the real app from there. The design system tokens above are ready to carry directly into the production build.
